The AI Transformation Framework

GenAI Solution Development is a program that lasts between 3 to 12 months.

Learn and enable

1-2 weeks

Identify and evaluate high-impact AI use cases

Prioritize and prototype

3-4 weeks

Select your use case and build a working PoC to validate assumptions

Build and integrate

2-6 months

Develop a custom-made AI solution integrated with your data and tailored to your business objectives.

Optimize and scale

2-6 months

Scale the solution and develop in-house AI capabilities to maintain and augment.
